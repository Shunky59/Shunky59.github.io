Prusa MK4s + MMU3 SolidWorks G-Code Modification

The Setup & Applications

My workshop centers around a Prusa MK4s equipped with the MMU3 multi-material unit. To ensure industrial-grade reliability for warp-prone materials like ABS and ASA, I designed a custom temperature-controlled enclosure.

Custom-built thermal enclosure with fume extraction.
Prototyping suspension components for Lehigh Formula SAE.

Mechatronics: Pen Plotter Conversion

To experiment with CNC motion paths beyond traditional filament printing, I designed a modular attachment that converts the 3D printer into a precision 2D pen plotter. This required modifying G-code generation scripts to alter Z-hop behavior for drawing instruments.

Custom carriage design modeled in Fusion 360 to hold various drafting tools.

Impact: COVID-19 Face Shield Response

In March 2020, I converted my workshop into a 24/7 manufacturing line to address the PPE shortage. Coordinating with local hospitals, I produced and delivered face shields to front-line workers.
