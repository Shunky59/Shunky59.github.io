3D Printer - Prusa MK4s + MMU3

Hardware | G-Code | Calibration | Assembly

Project Overview

This project involved the complete assembly, calibration, and modification of a Prusa MK4s 3D printer with the MMU3 (Multi-Material Upgrade). The goal was to establish a reliable rapid-prototyping station capable of printing complex geometries in multiple materials.

Technical Challenges

  • MMU3 Integration: Calibrated the buffer and selector to handle filament swaps without jamming.
  • Precision Assembly: Squared the frame to within 0.1mm tolerance to ensure dimensional accuracy of printed parts.
  • Material Tuning: Tuned profiles in PrusaSlicer for PLA, PETG, and TPU.

Outcome

The printer is now the workhorse for my other engineering projects, including parts for the Autonomous Medical Robot and Drone chassis components.
